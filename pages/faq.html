Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about QuickBasket

What is QuickBasket?

QuickBasket is your go-to online grocery delivery service that brings fresh groceries, snacks, and household essentials right to your doorstep in just minutes. We partner with local stores to ensure you get the freshest products quickly and conveniently.

How fast is the delivery?

We pride ourselves on lightning-fast delivery! Most orders are delivered within 45-60 minutes of placing your order. Delivery times may vary based on your location and order volume during peak hours.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to select areas across major cities in India. You can check if we deliver to your location by entering your pincode on our homepage. We're constantly expanding our service areas, so check back if we're not in your area yet!

Is there a minimum order value?

No, there's no minimum order value! You can order as little or as much as you need. However, some delivery charges may apply for orders below â‚¹199.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, digital wallets, and cash on delivery. All online payments are processed securely through encrypted payment gateways.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it. After that, the order goes into preparation and cannot be modified. Please contact our customer support immediately if you need assistance.

How do I apply a coupon code?

You can apply coupon codes during checkout. Simply enter the code in the "Apply Coupon" field and click apply. The discount will be reflected in your final amount. Check our app regularly for the latest offers and deals!

What if I receive a damaged or wrong product?

We take quality very seriously. If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact our customer support within 24 hours with photos of the item. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund immediately.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes! In fact, we offer delivery within the hour for most orders. Our goal is to get your groceries to you as quickly as possible, typically within 45-60 minutes.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive real-time updates via SMS and email. You can also track your order status in the "My Orders" section of your account.

Still have questions?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Our customer support team is here to help!
