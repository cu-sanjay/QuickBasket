Shipping Policy

Fast, reliable delivery to your doorstep

1. Delivery Areas

QuickBasket currently delivers to select areas across major cities in India. You can check if we deliver to your location by entering your pincode on our homepage or during checkout.

We are constantly expanding our service areas. If we don't currently deliver to your location, please check back soon or sign up for our newsletter to be notified when we expand to your area.

2. Delivery Time

We pride ourselves on lightning-fast delivery:

  • Standard Delivery: 45-60 minutes from order confirmation
  • Express Delivery: 30-45 minutes (available in select areas)
  • Scheduled Delivery: Choose your preferred time slot (2-hour windows available)

Delivery times may vary during peak hours, adverse weather conditions, or high-demand periods like festivals and holidays.

3. Delivery Charges

Orders above ₹199 FREE Delivery
Orders below ₹199 ₹29 Delivery Fee
Express Delivery ₹49 Additional

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is confirmed, you can track it in real-time:

  • Receive SMS and email notifications at each stage
  • Track your delivery partner's location on the map
  • Get estimated arrival time updates
  • Contact your delivery partner directly if needed

5. Delivery Process

Here's what happens after you place your order:

  1. Order Confirmation: You'll receive an instant confirmation via SMS and email
  2. Order Preparation: Our partner store picks and packs your items carefully
  3. Quality Check: Items are checked for freshness and quality
  4. Out for Delivery: Your order is assigned to a delivery partner
  5. Delivery: Your order arrives at your doorstep

6. Delivery Instructions

You can add special delivery instructions during checkout:

  • Gate code or building access information
  • Preferred drop-off location
  • Contact person details
  • Any specific handling requirements

7. Failed Delivery

If our delivery partner is unable to deliver your order:

  • They will attempt to contact you via phone
  • If unreachable, they will wait for 10 minutes at the delivery location
  • You can reschedule delivery for a convenient time
  • If the order cannot be delivered, you'll receive a full refund

8. Contactless Delivery

For your safety and convenience, we offer contactless delivery:

  • Select "Contactless Delivery" during checkout
  • Your order will be left at your doorstep
  • You'll receive a notification when it's delivered
  • Payment must be made online for contactless delivery

9. Product Inspection

We encourage you to inspect your order upon delivery:

  • Check for any damaged or incorrect items
  • Verify the quantity and quality of products
  • Report any issues immediately to our customer support
  • Take photos if there are any concerns

10. Special Handling

Certain products require special handling:

  • Frozen Items: Delivered in insulated packaging
  • Fragile Items: Extra care taken during packing and delivery
  • Fresh Produce: Delivered within optimal temperature range
  • Dairy Products: Maintained in cold chain throughout delivery

11. Holidays and Peak Times

During festivals, holidays, and peak shopping periods:

  • Delivery times may be slightly longer than usual
  • We recommend placing orders in advance
  • Express delivery may not be available
  • You'll be notified of any delays via SMS/email

Need Help with Delivery?

If you have questions about your delivery or need to make changes to your order, contact us immediately.
