Returns & Refunds Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority

1. Our Commitment

At QuickBasket, we are committed to delivering fresh, high-quality products. If you're not completely satisfied with your order, we're here to help with returns and refunds.

2. Return Eligibility

You can request a return or refund if:

  • Products are damaged, defective, or expired
  • Wrong items were delivered
  • Items are missing from your order
  • Products don't match the description
  • Quality issues with fresh produce, dairy, or other perishables

Important: Returns must be requested within 24 hours of delivery.

3. Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned once delivered:

  • Products that have been opened or used
  • Items without original packaging
  • Perishable items that show no quality issues
  • Personal care and hygiene products (unless defective)
  • Items damaged due to misuse or negligence

4. How to Request a Return

Follow these simple steps to request a return:

  1. Contact Us: Call +91 9800800808 or email hello@quickbasket.com within 24 hours
  2. Provide Details: Share your order number and reason for return
  3. Submit Photos: Send clear photos of the damaged or incorrect items
  4. Verification: Our team will review your request within 2-4 hours
  5. Approval: Once approved, we'll arrange pickup or process your refund

5. Refund Process

Once your return is approved, we'll process your refund:

Approval

Return request approved within 2-4 hours

Refund Initiated

Refund processed within 24 hours

Amount Credited

Funds appear in your account within 5-7 business days

6. Refund Methods

Refunds will be processed using the same payment method used for the original purchase:

  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-7 business days
  • UPI/Digital Wallet: 3-5 business days
  • Net Banking: 5-7 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: Bank transfer within 7-10 business days

For COD orders, we'll need your bank account details to process the refund.

7. Replacement Options

Instead of a refund, you can choose to:

  • Instant Replacement: Get the same product delivered again (subject to availability)
  • Store Credit: Receive credit in your QuickBasket wallet for future orders
  • Alternative Product: Choose a different product of equal or lesser value

8. Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds:

  • When only some items in your order have issues
  • For minor quality concerns that don't warrant a full return
  • When products are partially damaged but still usable

Our customer support team will work with you to find a fair solution.

9. Order Cancellation

You can cancel your order before it's dispatched:

  • Within 5 minutes: Full refund, no questions asked
  • After 5 minutes: Contact customer support immediately
  • After dispatch: Cancellation not possible, but you can refuse delivery

Refunds for cancelled orders are processed within 24-48 hours.

10. Quality Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our products:

  • All fresh produce is hand-picked and quality-checked
  • Expiry dates are verified before dispatch
  • Products are stored and transported at optimal temperatures
  • Packaging is done carefully to prevent damage
